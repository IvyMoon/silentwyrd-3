    Mr. Fate

Fate be strange...fate be silent.

Mr. Fate has existed for millenia. He influences the world with the stroke of his invisible hand.

His nature is capricious.
One moment, he wreaks havoc...the very next, he provides good fortune for some silly billy.

Today, Mr. Fate watches humanity with mockful eyes. They know nothing, those simpletons.
"Fools!" he scoffs out loud. "I pity you all."
Nobody hears him. Silence abounds.
Mr. Fate grows angry at it.

He decides to disparage the universe.

"Behold! The simpleminded sun! Tis inglorious—it fuses hydrogen mindlessly.
It cares not for what it does. Must we honor its glory when its fusion is so ignoble?"
Mr. Fate, disgusted with the sun, puts on his sunglasses and faces the moon.

"Hark! The moon! Tis pure stupidity."
He bends over and flashes his buttocks at a crater.
To hell with decency.

"Observe! Down below—a contemptible cheat."
On earth, a man searches for the fountain of youth.
"Thou mayest not live forever, my mortal friend."
Mr. Fate silently strikes down the moron.
The man's quest is now ended.

Mr. Fate has a dark sense of humor. It's too bad nobody can hear him speak.
But by the stroke of luck, modern society has developed the Internet.

Mr. Fate's words are silent. But the Internet gives him a voice.
His stories are now wired on Silent Wyrd for all to hear.
